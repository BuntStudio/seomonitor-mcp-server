The setup

Three steps, two minutes.

1

Copy your API key

In SEOmonitor, open your account settings and copy your API key. The assistant sees exactly the campaigns your account can see — nothing more.

2

Add the connector

Your personal connector URL is
mcp.seomonitor.com/YOUR_API_KEY/mcp
Pick your assistant below for exact steps.

3

Ask a question

“Which keywords gained AI Overview presence this month?” · “Compare my brand across ChatGPT, Gemini and Perplexity.” · “Draft an outline for my top topic recommendation.”

Claude Desktop & claude.ai

Available on paid Claude plans (desktop app and claude.ai on the web).

  1. Open Settings → Connectors
  2. Click Add custom connector
  3. Name it SEOmonitor and paste your connector URL: 
    https://mcp.seomonitor.com/YOUR_API_KEY/mcp
  4. Save. The tools appear in the composer's connector menu — the first use of each tool asks for your approval.

Claude Code

One command in your terminal — --scope user makes it available in every project:

claude mcp add --transport http --scope user seomonitor \
  https://mcp.seomonitor.com/YOUR_API_KEY/mcp

Check it's connected with claude mcp list · remove any time with claude mcp remove seomonitor. Tools are available in your next session.

ChatGPT

Works today via Developer mode — Plus, Pro, Business, Enterprise and Edu plans (web).

  1. Open Settings → Connectors → Advanced settings and turn on Developer mode
  2. Back in Connectors, choose Create (add a custom connector)
  3. Name it SEOmonitor, paste your connector URL, choose No authentication (your key is already in the URL), save: 
    https://mcp.seomonitor.com/YOUR_API_KEY/mcp
  4. In a chat, enable the connector from the composer's tools menu and ask away. Sign-in with your SEOmonitor account (no key handling) arrives with our upcoming public ChatGPT listing.

Codex CLI

Add the server to your Codex config, then restart Codex:

# ~/.codex/config.toml
[mcp_servers.seomonitor]
url = "https://mcp.seomonitor.com/YOUR_API_KEY/mcp"

Verify with codex mcp list. Prefer local? The downloadable stdio package is in the developer docs.

Any MCP client

The connector speaks standard MCP Streamable HTTP — point any compliant client or agent framework at your URL. No headers or extra auth setup; the key travels in the URL. Quick health check from a terminal:

curl -X POST https://mcp.seomonitor.com/YOUR_API_KEY/mcp \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -H "Accept: application/json, text/event-stream" \
  -d '{"jsonrpc":"2.0","id":1,"method":"initialize","params":{"protocolVersion":"2024-11-05","capabilities":{},"clientInfo":{"name":"check","version":"1"}}}'

A JSON answer naming seomonitor-mcp-server means you're connected.

Treat your connector URL like a password. It contains your API key and grants read access to your account's campaign data. Don't paste it into shared documents or group chats. Regenerating your API key disconnects every assistant that uses the old URL.

The toolkit

What you can ask.

Rankings & campaigns

  • List your campaigns — every active campaign and its setup
  • Campaign overview at a glance — visibility, average rank and movements in one answer
  • Top & bottom keywords — your best and worst performers, ranked
  • Search your tracked keywords — filter the whole set, exact counts included

AI Overviews (Google)

  • Daily AI Overview ranks — desktop & mobile, with brand presence
  • Competitors inside AI Overviews — who else appears, and where
  • Brand-mention visibility — how often AI Overviews mention you
  • Citation visibility — how often your site is cited
  • Your most-cited pages — which pages AI answers link to

AI Search engines

  • AI Search answers for your keywords — what ChatGPT, Gemini & Perplexity say
  • Engine-by-engine performance — your brand compared across the three
  • Where your brand shows up — top keywords with AI Search presence
  • Daily AI Search ranks — your site's position in AI answers
  • Mentions & citations over time — group-level AI Search visibility

AI Writer

  • Topic recommendations — what to write next, per campaign
  • Check a generation in progress — status of a running request
  • Read your drafts & live articles — outline, AI version, working draft, live

AI Writer actions require an account with AI Writer enabled.
